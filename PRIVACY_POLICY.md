Privacy Policy — Connected Dreams A

Effective Date: November 2025

Manisha Shah (“we,” “us,” or “our”) developed Connected Dreams A ("the App").
This privacy policy explains how the App handles user data.
Connected Dreams A runs entirely on-device, without collecting, storing, or transmitting personal information.

1. No Data Collection

Connected Dreams A does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.
We do not use analytics, trackers, advertising tools, or third-party data processors.

All app functionality performs locally on your device.

2. Bluetooth Usage

The App may request permission to use Bluetooth for these optional features:

Activating or communicating with nearby Bluetooth-enabled devices (e.g., audio or lighting accessories)

Allowing the user to integrate external devices for wellness or health-related tracking

Bluetooth communication remains local.
No device information, identifiers, or connection data gets uploaded or shared.

3. Camera Usage

The App may request Camera access to enable:

QR code scanning

Optional document text scanning (for user journaling)

Optional Matter pairing in future releases

Connected Dreams A does not record, store, transmit, or upload images or video.

—

4. Journaling & User-Generated Content

Users may optionally enter short text entries.
All entries get stored locally on device and are not transmitted or shared.
You may delete your entries at any time within the App.

5. No Account Required

The App does not use accounts or logins.
We do not request personal information such as names, emails, or passwords.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App does not collect data from any user, including children.
However, parents should supervise device use consistent with Apple’s policies.

7. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party software development kits (SDKs), analytics services, or external data processors.

8. Data Retention

All data remains on-device and fully under your control.
Deleting the app deletes all stored content.
